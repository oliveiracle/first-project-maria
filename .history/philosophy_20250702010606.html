Our Story: A Legacy of Love

The story of Maria didn't start with a business plan, but with a feeling: a heartfelt sense of longing and remembrance.

The name we chose is a tribute to my mother, who passed away in 1995. Describing her is simple, yet profound: she was sweet, calm, and above all pure love. Her essence wasn't found in grand speeches, but in the peace she conveyed and the care in her gestures.

These were the values she left me as the richest inheritance. And they are the pillars of our philosophy. We want every product and every experience with Maria to be a direct reflection of that legacy: an invitation to calm amidst the routine, a gesture of sweetness for oneself and for others, and an act of love in its purest form.
